Three Plans, One Caring Experience

PLANS DESIGNED TO MEET THE NEEDS OF EVERYONE

Medi-Cal

No-cost health coverage for qualified residents. Comprehensive benefits including medical, dental, vision, and prescription coverage.

DualChoice

No-cost, integrated health plan for people with Medicare and Medi-Cal coverage. Coordinated care for your unique needs.

Covered California

Low-cost health coverage program for those who don't quite qualify for Medi-Cal. Quality care for you and your family.

24-Hour Nurse Advice Line

Get free medical information and advice 24 hours a day, 365 days a year from qualified healthcare professionals.

Transportation Services

We provide round-trip transportation for members to medical appointments. It's easier that way.

Vision Services

We cover both eye exams as well as eyeglasses and contact lenses, when needed. Complete vision care for the whole family.
