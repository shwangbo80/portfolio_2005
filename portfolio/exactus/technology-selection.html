SERVICES / Technology Selection

Exactus offers software selection services that are predicated on the needs of the client in lieu of previously established interlocking directorates. Many of our competitors have established relationships with technology providers that impede their ability to provide a true objective analysis and recommendation. At Exactus we are objective to technology. Exactus has established relationships with dozens of real estate technology providers, and our decision to recommend technology is always based on the needs of the client.

  • Interview real estate data stakeholders including representatives from corporate real estate, legal, risk management, accounting, finance, executive and facility management departments.

  • Determine needs and requirements of all users.

  • Provide client with detailed findings that identify issues and recommendations that cite potential benefits and risks.

  • Provide a ROI (Return on Investment) analysis to justify the procurement of new technology.

  • Review abstracts for accuracy utilizing a proven independent quality assurance program and make corrections as necessary.
