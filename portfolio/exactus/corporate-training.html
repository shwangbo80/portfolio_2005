SERVICES / Corporate Training

Exactus’ training program is deigned to provide our clients lease administration staff with the best practices to effectively manage their ongoing lease management programs. Our training is documented to be cost-effective, with specific focus on the limitation of corporate liability. All too often our client’s lease administration staff are not empowered with the knowledge and tools to provide best-in-class services, resulting in additional costs that are not readily identified.

  • Tailor training programs for the specific needs of the client (e.g., lease administration processes, lease auditing, etc.)

  • Provide presentation materials.

  • Conduct seminar with Q&A session.

  • Offer monthly collaboration conference calls to establish a sense of unity among geographically dispersed lease analysts and property accountants.
