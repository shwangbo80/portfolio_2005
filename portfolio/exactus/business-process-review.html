SERVICES / Business Process Review

Exactus’ business process review services are designed to provide us with a comprehensive understanding of the existing processes of a client prior to making a technology or process recommendation. We believe that there are inherent advantages to operational efficiency and processes with a change in technology, and that those advantages can only be fully understood with a comprehension of the existing operations. The principals of Exactus have excelled in identifying operational process savings above and beyond the traditional reduction of operating expense payments to landlords.

  • Interview real estate data stakeholders to assess current processes.

  • Interview departments that are dependent upon real estate information to assess whether dissemination of information is effective and efficient.

  • Provide client with detailed findings that identify issues, document the flow of information and processes, and make recommendations that cite potential benefits and risks, and actionable solutions. The report of findings will focus on streamlining operations and will be accompanied by a ROI (return on investment) analysis if applicable.
