Flow AI Project

About
Flow AI is an intelligent workflow automation platform designed to help modern teams streamline repetitive tasks, integrate business tools, and boost productivity with AI-powered suggestions.

Goal
Design a high-converting landing page for Flow AI that clearly communicates its value and drives user signups for early access.

Role
UI/UX designer and front-end developer responsible for layout, copywriting, responsive design, and visual branding.

Features
Hero section, product highlights, benefits grid, testimonial slider, and a prominent email signup form.

Tools
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
