Heart of Christ Fellowship Church Website

About
Heart of Christ Fellowship Church (HOCFC) is a vibrant, Korean Christian community based in Tustin, California. The church is dedicated to worship, outreach, and spiritual growth, offering services in both Korean and English and supporting local and global missions.

Goal
Design and develop a welcoming, informative website using Wordpress that shares HOCFC's mission, worship times, announcements, and ministry opportunities, while making it easy for visitors to connect, participate, and give online.

Role
UI/UX designer and front-end developer responsible for branding, layout, copywriting, responsive design, and building the site on WordPress, including integrating features such as sermon archives.

Features
Worship schedule, announcements, location map, photo gallery, sermon archive, online giving, contact form, and social media integration (Instagram, YouTube).

Tools
PHP, WordPress, and custom graphics.
