IEHP Project

About
IEHP (Inland Empire Health Plan) is a community-based health plan serving the Inland Empire region of California. As a public entity, IEHP provides comprehensive healthcare coverage through three main plans: Medi-Cal, DualChoice, and Covered California, ensuring accessible healthcare for diverse community needs.

Goal
Design and develop a user-friendly, accessible website that clearly communicates IEHP's health plan options, benefits, and community resources while making it easy for residents to understand their coverage options and enroll in appropriate plans.

Role
UI/UX designer and front-end developer responsible for creating an intuitive navigation system, responsive design implementation, accessibility compliance, and clear presentation of complex healthcare information.

Features
Plan comparison tools, provider search functionality, member portal integration, multilingual support, community wellness center locator, and comprehensive benefits information with clear calls-to-action for enrollment.

Tools
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
