Combishades Project

About
Combishades® is a leader in innovative window shading systems, offering revolutionary design concepts and high-quality products for home and business decoration. Their solutions combine style, safety, and advanced technology to enhance any space.

Goal
Design and develop a modern, informative website to showcase Combishades®'s unique product lines, highlight their cord-free and child-safe solutions, and support both customers and fabricator partners in learning about and purchasing their products.

Role
UI/UX designer and front-end developer responsible for branding, layout, copywriting, responsive design, and integrating product galleries, partner application forms, and contact features.

Features
Product showcase (Combi Shade®, Roller Shade, Triple Shade™, Veilette™), intuitive fabric preview, fabricator partner program, cord-free child safety information, consultation booking, contact form, and business information.

Tools
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP, and custom graphics.
